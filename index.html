Collaboration with Mandi Barrus and Liz on a song Cycle

I'm Excited for this chance to work with a couple of really talanted artists for an upcoming project. Mandi Barrus is a Mezzo Soprano that most recently earned her MM in vocal performance at the University of Utah. She has a long career as a performer including at Utah Opera, and Hale Center theatre. We will be adapting and setting some texts from lyricist Liz {name}. Many of which center on themes of renewel , journeying, and struggle from a woman's perspective. There will likely be a performance through Opera Contempo before the end of the Summer. Stay Tuned.

Chamber Opera with Opera Contempo

I will be composing a chamber Opera to be premeired in March 2021. The Libretto will be written by Jennifer Campbell, who I have previosly worked with on a song Cycle and who is an extremely talented writer. The vision of Opera Contempo is to create space for new composers to debut new works about contemporary topics. I can't reveal too much about the plot, but it centers around artificial intelligence and what it means to be human vs other. Look for updates and insights about the process on my blog as the project progresses.
